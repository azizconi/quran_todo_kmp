# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Quran Todo (Quran Hifz)** ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use the mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little data as possible.

- **App data you create**: learning progress, statuses, goals, settings, and related study records.
- **Audio input (microphone)**: if you use recitation features, the App requests microphone access to process your recitation.
- **Technical data**: basic device or app diagnostics may be generated by the operating system for stability and troubleshooting.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use data to:

- provide core app functionality (memorization, review, tracking, and settings),
- enable recitation features (when microphone permission is granted),
- download Quran content and related resources from the internet,
- improve reliability and app performance.

## 3. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

- `RECORD_AUDIO`: used only for recitation/listening functionality.
- `INTERNET`: used to download Quran content, translations, audio, and app resources.

## 4. Data Storage

- Most study data is stored locally on your device.
- We do not sell your personal data.
- If optional cloud or backup features are enabled in future versions, this policy will be updated.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties for advertising purposes.

Data may be processed by service providers only when required for app functionality (for example, content delivery or network infrastructure).

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App may be used by students under 13 only with parent/guardian and teacher guidance where required.
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children beyond what is necessary for app functionality.
If you believe a child has provided personal information inappropriately, contact us and we will review and remove it if applicable.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

- Local app data remains on your device until you remove it or uninstall the App.
- You can clear app data through device settings.
- If cloud-related features are introduced later, deletion options will be documented here.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a revised "Last updated" date.
